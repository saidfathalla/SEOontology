SWRL rules

Rule #1back to ToC

colocated withop(?e1,?e2) , has location(?e1,?l1) -> has location(?e2,?l1)

Rule #2back to ToC

agent(?a) , holds role(?a,?e) -> participates inop(?a,?e)

Rule #3back to ToC

has location(?e1,?l1) , has location(?e2,?l2) , end date(?e1,?ed1) , end date(?e2,?ed2) , start date(?e1,?sd1) , start date(?e2,?sd2) , equal(?ed1,?ed2) , equal(?l1,?l2) , equal(?sd1,?sd2) -> colocated withop(?e1,?e2)

Rule #4back to ToC

colocated withop(?e1,?e2) , has location(?e2,?l) -> has location(?e1,?l)
